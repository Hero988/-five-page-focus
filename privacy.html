Data handling

Privacy notice

Last updated: 22 July 2026.

Who is responsible

Suliman Tadros is the data controller, trading as Five Page Focus, 2 Rosecroft Gardens, London NW2 6HX. Email privacy questions, rights requests or complaints to gissuliman@gmail.com.

Information we use

For an order or enquiry we may use a business contact's name, work email, organisation, payment/order reference, five supplied public URLs, page goals, authority confirmation, correspondence, report and support record. Evidence may incidentally capture personal information already visible on a public page; we minimise or redact it unless needed to explain a finding.

Do not send passwords, private URLs, customer records, payment details, health information, special-category data or other sensitive material.

Why we use it

  • Contract: to answer an order-related request, validate scope, deliver the review and provide support or a refund.
  • Legal obligation: to retain payment, refund and tax records required by law.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure the service, establish delivery facts, prevent misuse and handle complaints or legal claims proportionately.

Service providers and international processing

  • Stripe hosts checkout and processes payment/fraud information. Five Page Focus does not receive full card details.
  • Google provides the Gmail support and delivery mailbox.
  • Apify receives only the authorised public URLs needed to run bounded Lighthouse evidence.
  • OpenAI may process redacted evidence summaries used to help organise and quality-check report text. Raw screenshots and unredacted personal data are not intentionally submitted.
  • GitHub Pages hosts this static site and may keep security access logs. Five Page Focus uses no site analytics, advertising scripts, forms, cookies or remote fonts.

These providers may process information outside the UK under their published safeguards. We do not sell personal information.

How long we keep it

  • Live project evidence, final deliverables and ordinary support material: up to 30 days after delivery, then deleted unless a live complaint or legal claim requires the minimum relevant record.
  • Unconverted enquiries: up to 12 months after the last meaningful contact.
  • Invoices, payments, refunds and necessary tax records: for the period required by UK law, normally at least five years after the relevant 31 January tax-return deadline.

An opaque order reference and delivery hashes may be retained without page evidence to reconcile finance records. Providers may retain limited backup, fraud or security records under their own schedules.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, UK data-protection law may give you rights to be informed, access and correct information, ask for deletion or restriction, object to processing and receive portable information. These rights are not absolute. There is no solely automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

Email gissuliman@gmail.com to exercise a right. We may request proportionate identity confirmation and normally respond within one month.

Complaints

Email gissuliman@gmail.com with “Data protection complaint” and the order reference. You can also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office.
