2. Service and price

The fixed price is £199 GBP for one website and exactly five authorised, public HTTPS page URLs. No VAT is charged. Payment is taken through Stripe. The service includes:

  • axe-core and Lighthouse evidence plus bounded manual review of keyboard access, focus, structure, labels, link purpose, meaningful image alternatives, safe form validation, contrast warnings, zoom/reflow and relevant sampled dynamic content;

3. Intake and authority

After payment, the buyer must complete the safe intake, name five pages on one hostname and confirm that they control the website or have the controller's permission to commission testing. Pages must require no login or bypass and must contain no private, customer, patient, employee, payment or other sensitive data.

We may reject unsafe or out-of-scope material. The buyer may substitute eligible pages on the same website. If valid intake is not supplied within seven days of a request to correct it, we will cancel and refund the order.

4. Delivery

The 48-hour delivery period begins only when both payment and valid intake are confirmed. Delivery is by email to the checkout contact. A redirect, access block, outage or material page change may pause the clock while a replacement page is requested. If we cannot deliver because of our systems or no eligible replacement is possible, we will issue a prompt full refund.

5. Important boundaries

This is a bounded accessibility readiness review, not a formal accessibility audit, legal opinion, certification, accessibility statement approval, VPAT/ACR, or guarantee of WCAG or European Accessibility Act compliance. Automated tools cannot determine accessibility or find every barrier. The review does not establish whole-site or whole-process conformance.

Authenticated/private states, documents such as PDFs, native apps, code remediation, penetration testing, disabled-user research and broad assistive-technology compatibility testing are excluded unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

6. Evidence, AI and confidentiality

AI helps organise redacted evidence, draft descriptions and check consistency. Every delivered finding is reviewed against captured page evidence; no finding is based on AI output alone. We do not send raw screenshots or unredacted personal data to the AI service.

We use project information only to deliver and support the order, subject to the privacy notice. Live project material stays outside the public code repository. We do not publish the buyer's report or use it as a case study without separate written permission.
